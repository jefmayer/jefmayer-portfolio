AmplifyIt In-store Experience

Overview

The in-store experience showcased the connectivity of the new Samsung Galaxy Tab and Beats headphones. Several audio and visual components needed to be quickly fabricated and programmed for the grand opening of the Verizon Michigan Avenue flagship store.

Solution

Our team created a custom sampler app and a 9-channel mixing board guests used to personalize their mixes. Layering samples and adjusting levels altered the playback of musician videos on a massive 9-screen display. Once finished, mixes were shared to an online playlist.

Galaxy Share To Go Kiosk

Overview

Samsung installed 65″ touchscreen kiosks at airports to promote the Galaxy’s NFC capabilities. The kiosk would quickly transfer content to travelers’ devices. I developed the kiosk software and brought the experience to life.

Solution

Working across project teams in multiple cities, I coded an Actionscript application to drive the graphics-intense interface, stream videos, and easily facilitate the NFC transfer between the app and users’ phones.

Graber Consumer Website + Dealer Portal

Overview

Graber, a high-quality, custom window treatment brand sold through dealers, needed an engaging site that allowed consumers to explore their full range of solutions and generated leads for affiliated dealers, who could then respond and turn those leads into customers.

Solution

We positioned the 80-year old Graber brand as a trusted industry leader, building on its legacy of innovation with an updated visual system, refreshed marketing collateral, a responsive dealer portal with real-time sales tools, and a replatformed, redesigned consumer site.
