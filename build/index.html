AmplifyIt In-store Experience

Overview

The in-store experience showcased the connectivity of the new Samsung Galaxy Tab and Beats headphones. Several audio and visual components needed to be quickly fabricated and programmed for the grand opening of the Verizon Michigan Avenue flagship store.

Solution

Our team created a custom sampler app and a 9-channel mixing board guests used to personalize their mixes. Layering samples and adjusting levels altered the playback of musician videos on a massive 9-screen display. Once finished, mixes were shared to an online playlist.

Galaxy Share To Go Kiosk

Overview

Samsung installed 65″ touchscreen kiosks at several US airports to promote the Galaxy’s NFC capabilities. Travelers could browse an ever-changing content library and download selections to their devices.

Solution

Working with a project team split between Chicago and Austin, I developed software to drive the graphics-intense interface, stream videos, and easily facilitate the NFC transfer between the kiosk and users’ phones.

Trainspotted React App

Overview

Aside from the signal clearance between Chicago commuter trains, there was no set schedule to the freight trains that rumbled by outside my office window. Even so, I wondered if it was possible to observe freight trains making a return trip and to find the average time between repeated sightings.

Solution

I built an app using MERN with Redux and an admin to enter field notes about each train. With the data, I mapped out a data visualization according to day, time, and train line along with a dashboard that sliced and aggregated the data based on the frequency and resightings of each engine.
