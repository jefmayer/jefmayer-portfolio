AmplifyItIn-store Experience

Overview

AKQA contracted our shop to execute the AmplifyIt Beatmaker experience promoting the new Samsung Galaxy Tab and Beats headphones. Several custom, networked components needed to be programmed and built under a tight deadline tied to the grand opening of the Verizon flagship store on Michigan Avenue.

Solution

Our team developed a custom sampler app connected to a nine-channel mixing board, fabricated in-house. Guests mixed a personalized soundtrack and a massive 9-screen, 4K display ran stylized videos of musicians playing each sample. Adjusting the levels altered the videos’ size and added effects in real-time. Once finished, guests could share their mixes to an online playlist.

GalaxyShare To Go Kiosk

Overview

As part of a Galaxy product launch, Samsung installed 65″ touchscreen kiosks at airports. They promoted the phone’s NFC capabilities by quickly delivering content to travelers. My agency was brought in to develop the kiosk software and bring the experience to life.

Solution

Working across project teams in multiple cities, I coded an Actionscript application to drive the graphics-intense interface, stream videos, and easily facilitate the NFC transfer between the app and users’ phones.

GraberWebsite + Dealer Portal

Overview

Graber is a high-quality, custom window treatment brand sold primarily through dealers. They needed a site that allowed consumers to explore their full range of solutions and generated leads for affiliated dealers, who could then respond and turn those leads into customers.

Solution

We positioned the 80-year old Graber brand as a trusted industry leader, building on its legacy of innovation with an updated visual system, refreshed marketing collateral, a responsive dealer portal with real-time sales tools, and a replatformed, redesigned consumer site.
